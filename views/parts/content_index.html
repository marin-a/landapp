Bilice - Šibenik

Bilice is a municipality in the Sibenik-Knin county. It's population increased by 17% in the last 8 years. The town is predicted to be one of the major tourist attractions in Middle Dalmatia. It's advantageous position, at the sea, in mountainous terrain between national parks (Kornati, Plitvice and Krka) makes Bilice popular for both high-end and nautical tourism.

According to the Master Plan of the municipality and such investments groups like Real Treuhand Immobilien (Raiffeisen Bank Group) and IGC Real Estate following investments are going to be made:

  • On 14 hectares a hotel with 1400 beds and villas of higher category are going to be built in addition with a sports and recreational centre and restaurants
  • Beach is going to be extended by 700m
  • Building of a commercial port for yachts up to 50m length / marina with 200 berths
  • Residential buildings
  • Bank offices
  • Green areas, parks and children's playgrounds
  • Ambulant station
  • Postal office
  • Pharmacy
  • Primary school
  • Kindergarten
  • Walkways in the bay of Stubalj
The Sibenik-Knin County has the 3rd biggest arrival of nautical tourism and the most traffic of yachts, on the Adriatic. This indicates the huge potential for exclusive, high class tourism.
